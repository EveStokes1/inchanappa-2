Our Story

Inchanappa Equestrian Center began with a simple passion for horses and a commitment to providing them with a safe, well-managed home. What started as a small yard has grown into a welcoming space for both horses and riders, where care, respect, and good horsemanship are at the heart of everything we do. Our facilities are designed to give horses the comfort and freedom they need, whether they are enjoying time in the paddocks or resting in clean, well-maintained stables. Over time, the yard has become a place where people can learn, improve their riding, and build a genuine connection with their horses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and well-run environment where both horses and riders can thrive. We aim to maintain the highest standards of horse welfare while offering quality facilities for training, riding, and everyday care. At Inchanappa Equestrian Center, we believe that good horsemanship is built on patience, respect, and understanding. By creating a friendly and professional atmosphere, we hope to encourage riders of all levels to grow in confidence, develop their skills, and enjoy the unique bond that comes from working with horses.
