    • Lessons

    At Inchanappa Equestrian, our lessons are designed to help riders of all ages and abilities build confidence, skill, and a strong partnership with the horse. Whether you are a complete beginner learning the basics or a more experienced rider looking to refine your technique, our structured lessons focus on correct riding position, horse care, and safe, effective communication with the horse. Sessions are delivered in a supportive and friendly environment, with an emphasis on steady progress, enjoyment, and developing a lifelong appreciation for horsemanship.

    livery

    Our livery service at Inchanappa Equestrian provides a safe, welcoming, and well-managed environment for both horses and owners. We offer high-quality daily care with well-maintained stables and facilities, ensuring every horse’s health, comfort, and wellbeing are a top priority. Our yard is a friendly and supportive place where horses can thrive, and owners can have peace of mind knowing their horses are cared for to a high standard in a professional setting.
